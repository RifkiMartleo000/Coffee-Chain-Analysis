## ☕ Robust Regression Analysis: Impact of Sales, Inventory & Marketing on Profit  
### **Case Study: Coffee Chain Dataset (2012–2013)**

This repository presents a **robust regression analysis** using **S-estimation** to evaluate the effects of **Sales, Inventory, and Marketing** on **Profit** in the **Coffee Chain dataset** from **2012–2013**.

---

### Repository Contents:
- **Data Description**  
- **Theoretical Background** (Robust Regression, S-estimator)  
- **Source Code** (`R`)  
- **Robust Regression Model**  

---

### Key Features:
- Outlier detection & handling  
- Robust parameter estimation (resistant to outliers)  
- Comparison with **Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)**  
- Visualizations: scatter plots, residuals, influence diagnostics

> **Key Insight**: Sales, inventory, and marketing have a significant impact on profits.

[Run in R](https://rpubs.com/leomartsys/SIM_CM1)
